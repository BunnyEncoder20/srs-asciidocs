== 1.4 STAKEHOLDERS AND CONCERNS

1. HQ/HOD, CO/Unit Admin, Users (operator, maintainer), Trainers, Trainee: 
   Hierarchical two-factor approvals, enforceable RBAC, and auditable access paths 
   across system/subsystem scopes.

   a. Operators, Maintainers: Access to relevant manuals, full-feature navigation, 
      search, and support.

   b. Trainers, Trainees: Training content, test creation/execution, analytics, and 
      a seamless user experience.

2. Audit, Security: End-to-end traceability of approvals, events, offline operation, 
   and compliance logging.
